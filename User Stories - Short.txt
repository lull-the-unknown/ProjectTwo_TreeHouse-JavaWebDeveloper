Minimum Required
------------------

Organizer
 - create new team
 - add players
 - remove players
 - teams listed alphabetically
 - players listed alphabetically with stats
 - view team report, players grouped by height
 - league balance report showing total count of experienced players vs. total count of inexperienced players

Coach
 - print team roster


Extra Credit
--------------

Administrator
 - not allowed to create more teams than there are players
 - players available for adding to team should not include players already added to a team
 - team report should show average experience level
 - League Balance Report should include count of players for each height
